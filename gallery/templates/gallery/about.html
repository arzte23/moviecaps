MovieCaps

MovieCaps is a curated digital gallery designed for cinema lovers and visual storytellers. It’s a space to explore the art of cinematography through high-quality screencaps, organized by mood, style, and genre.

Built with Django 6.0 and HTMX, this project focuses on a fast, seamless browsing experience and deep architectural exploration of modern backend development.
